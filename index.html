What is Fitness?

Fitness is a mix of cardiovascular endurance, muscular strength, muscular endurance, flexibility, and balance. It means having the physical capacity to perform everyday tasks, feel healthy, and reduce risk of disease. Fitness is built consistently with movement, progressive challenge, recovery, and good nutrition and sleep.
